How To Play

Move the snake, eat food, use powerups, and avoid hitting your own body.

  • Controls: Swipe on mobile. Keyboard: Arrow keys / WASD.
  • Wrap mode: Crossing an edge brings you from the opposite side.
  • Food gives +10 score and grows the snake.
  • Best score is saved on your device.

Powerups

  • Speed (yellow): Snake moves faster for 6 seconds.
  • Slow (blue): Snake moves slower for 6 seconds.
  • Bonus (purple): Instant +25 score.
  • Shrink (orange): Removes 3 tail cells (minimum length 3).
